Animation Principles

Demonstrating a Bouncing Ball

Principles Shown:

  • Squash & Stretch

    Notice how the ball stretches as it falls (gaining speed) and squashes flat when it hits the ground. This gives it a sense of mass and flexibility.

  • Timing & Spacing (Ease-In/Out)

    The ball accelerates due to gravity (spacing increases) and decelerates as it rises (spacing decreases). It "eases" (slows) into the peak of its arc before falling again.

  • Arcs

    While this is a simple vertical bounce, the physics engine creates a natural parabolic arc for the ball's movement over time.
